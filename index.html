Tic Tac Toe
play your move

Objective: Get three symbols in a row to win. Setup:Play on a 3x3 grid. Gameplay:placing your symbol on empty squares. Winning:Achieve three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Ending:Game ends when a player wins or the grid is full. Rematch:Start a new round for another chance to win. Enjoy!:Have fun playing Tic Tac Toe!
